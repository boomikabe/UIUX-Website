Introduction to UI/UX

Get an overview of UI/UX design principles and their importance in creating user-friendly interfaces.

What is UI Design?

UI design deals with the look and feel of a product's interface. It involves designing every visual element that users interact with, such as buttons, icons, colors, typography, and layout. UI designers aim to create interfaces that are aesthetically pleasing, consistent, and easy to navigate.

What is UX Design?

UX design focuses on the overall experience users have with a product. It encompasses the entire journey users take, from their first interaction with the product to accomplishing their goals. UX designers conduct research to understand user needs, create wireframes and prototypes to test ideas, and iterate based on feedback to optimize the user experience.
